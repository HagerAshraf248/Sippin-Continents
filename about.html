Our Journey

The story of how a backpacker's dream turned into a global cafe.

We began as a passionate dream along the beautiful banks of the Nile River in Cairo. We sought to perfectly blend Egypt's timeless, world-renowned hospitality with the extraordinary culinary diversity of the globe. From hidden coffee roasteries to iconic international street food, we brought the best of the world's flavors back to our home.

Today, our doors are open to locals and travelers alike, proudly serving artisan coffee and authentic global cuisines, carefully crafted to honor both their international roots and our beloved Egyptian heritage.
